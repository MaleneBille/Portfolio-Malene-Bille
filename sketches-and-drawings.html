The Effect of Photoshop

I recently had a wow experience when I first started working with photoshop. I have always loved to draw but the colors I could make with pencil and promarkers didn't pop out the way I wanted them too. Soon I realised that I can actually take a hand drawn/colored sketch and edit it in photoshop and get the color result I want. In the process of learning to photoshop images, I also learned to outline drawings and now I can fill in colors and shadows in photoshop without being distracted by the colors I did by hand.
